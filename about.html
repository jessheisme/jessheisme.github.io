I've always been a little disatisfied with doing what everyone else does (hence, not succumbing to a Squarespace template), and this mentality is definitely reflected in my work. Although I recognize the value in sticking with established patterns, I believe that true innovation can only come with the risk of challenging norms. With a reliance on intuition built upon grounded research and rapid prototyping, I strive to push borders and raise bars.



I was born in Texas, but I spent the majority of my life in Vancouver, Canada (yes, I'm a dual citizen). I recently graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a degree in Cognitive Science and Computer Science and am excited to make an impact on the world as a product designer!
