Jessica is a product designer who excels at breaking down complex problems and defining product vision. With a background in Cognitive Science and Computer Science, Jessica utilizes insights on human and social behavior to inform her designs and leverages her understanding of technical implementation to innovate within realistic means.


Jessica has been designing mobile apps and websites for over three years at companies such as Adobe, Avast, and Vimeo. Jessica is also a proud alum of the University of California, Berkeley, where she taught a popular Web Design class for over two years.


In her free time, Jessica enjoys traveling and documenting her life through film and photos.
