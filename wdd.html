Web Design Decal

Web Design Decal is a student-taught course that I co-instruct at UC Berkeley. Our class is unique because we emphasize strong design principles. We beieve that there is much more to making a website than simply the ability to code it. So while we do go over HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, we also make an effort to ensure that our students are developing a strong design foundation. We encourage our students to go through a design process of researching, prototyping, and testing and we go over various topics in UI/UX.
